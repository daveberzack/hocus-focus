Hocus Focus

Enhance your day

Every day, Hocus Focus presents a little riddle. A clue to find something in a hidden picture.

When you swipe around the canvas it will paint the picture with blocks of color. Swipe around quickly to paint big blocks that cover a wide area. Swipe slowly to reveal more details in a smaller area

Once you've identified the goal, click it to stop the timer. But don't make wild guesses. Every wrong click costs you a 10-second penalty.

You'll score up to five stars for beating the checkpoints shown in the timer. Some puzzles are tricky. Don't worry... you're supposed to feel a bit confused. After you win, it'll all make sense. You can share your score and paste it into a message or social media to play against friends.

Should you spend time getting the general context of a picture, or focus on an interesting area? Which region is worth exploring? What does that riddle mean, anyway? Are you confident enough to wager a guess? I don't know. I'm just the game designer.

Good hunting.
