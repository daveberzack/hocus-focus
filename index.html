How to play

Every day, there's a new riddle: a sly clue to find something in a hidden picture.

Paint the canvas with blocks of color. Paint over the blocks to reveal finer and finer details.

Once you've identified the objective, click it to stop the timer as quickly as you can. But be careful: every wrong click will cost you a 10-second penalty.

You'll score up to five stars for beating the checkpoints shown in the timer. Some puzzles are tricky and will take longer. You can share your score to play against friends.

Good hunting.
