How to play

Each daily quest gives a clue to find something in a hidden image as quickly as you can.

Swipe over the canvas to reveal the image with pixels of color. Move slower to paint finer details in a smaller area.

Try to find the answer as quickly as possible, then click on it to complete the quest. But if you're wrong, you get a 10-second penalty.

Good hunting!
