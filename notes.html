January 11 Update Notes

New puzzle Creator

I revised the editor tool, so it's easier to use, and no more holiday theme stuff. Just your own custom puzzle to share with friends. Try it now or click Create after you solve a puzzle.

Amped up Challenge

The brush is slower to paint fine details. You'll have to work harder to figure out what's happening behind the pixels. Also, the time goals will vary between puzzles.

Win Streak

I added a feature to track how many puzzles in a row you've won. Winning means getting at least one star.
